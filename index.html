Welcome to Draw and Shake, the innovative Android application that lets you create and share drawings with ease! Simply shake your device to erase your drawing and start fresh. It's a fun and interactive way to express your creativity.

Features of the application include:

  • Easy-to-use drawing interface
  • Shake to erase functionality
  • Multiple color and brush options
  • Save and share your drawings
